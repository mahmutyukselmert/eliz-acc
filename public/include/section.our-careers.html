The Heart of our Culture

Our Employees Are The Key To JRM's Success

As an employee owned company, we know that our dedicated employees are what make JRM one of the best general contracting & construction management companies in the industry. We strive to build a company we’re proud to call our own and believe in the importance of investing in people and their future. We offer our employees industry leading benefits from our employee stock ownership program to competitive salaries, mentoring from industry experts and advancement opportunities.

At JRM, we truly believe that the key to success is creating a positive work culture where our team of tight-knit employees foster open communication across all levels, from principals and directors to associates and junior professionals. Through our atmosphere of collaboration our team’s have completed a number of exciting high-profile projects including the Shops & Restaurants at Hudson Yards, One World Observatory, Major League Baseball’s Headquarters, along with projects for the NBA, Ferrari, Porsche, and SONY to name a few.
